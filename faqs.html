Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Drop smart box?
Drop smart box is a smart box to safely store all your deliveries, packages, and parcels with just a click of a button. All you need is our smart box installed outside your homes. With the help of our app, we will ensure you never miss a delivery again! A one-stop solution for hassle-free, contact-less deliveries.
How does it work?
Drop smart box is a user friendly system wherein, a delivery partner keys in a code (an OTP generated using your order ID), and places the parcel inside the box, and you get instantly notified. You can pick up your parcel as per your convenience, with just a click of a button on your phone that will instantly unlock the box. The camera on your smart delivery box also lets you see who made the delivery and what was delivered.
Why should you use a Drop smart box?
- To enjoy contactless parcel delivery with just a click of a button from your phone
- Convenient and hassle free
- User-friendly and easy to use
- No phone calls required
- 24/7 surveillance ensures that your packages are safe and free from thefts!
What are the models and sizes available?
We have two models
- 15 inches by 15 inches (currently available)
- 20 inches by 18.11 inches (coming soon)
What type of packages can be stored in the drop box?
Any package or parcel from as big as a 1 litre tetra-pack of juice to as big as a large 12 inch pizza box.
Where is it installed?
Anywhere convenient for you that requires a power source and wifi connection. We suggest that you place the box outside your door or gate to enjoy contactless parcel delivery.
Does the box have to be mounted?
We have two models, one of which requires mounting on the wall or the ground (suitable for independent houses) and another that does not. You can opt for the one that is convenient for you.
Who installs the box?
Our servicemen will install the box upon buying/subscribing the box and remove the same once the subscription is over.
Does it require a wifi connection?
Yes.
Is it waterproof?
Absolutely.
Can the box be unlocked from anywhere?
Yes, even if you are sitting in another country!
Does it accept deliveries from all platforms?
Yes.
